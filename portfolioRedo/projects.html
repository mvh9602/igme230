Astral Outbreak

2017. Astral Outbreak was a group project for GDAPS 2 during my first year at RIT. Within the group I was team lead. My responsibility was schedule meetings, manage documents, ensure progress, and help program. I mainly worked on enemy AI and helped the architect work on non UI programming. Created in monogame. In Astral Outbreak, you play as a stranded space citizen exploring a destroyed space ship in an attempt at survival. It is a short 2D shooter with ability upgrades and unique bosses.

Aquatic Adventure

2015. Aquatic Adventure was group project for an extracuricular technology group in my high school, called InterAlliance. In the group, I helped others work with programming in the jmonkey engine. I also worked on the AI for sharks. This was the first time working on a game for all of us. Aquatic Adventure is an educational game about the lifecycle of a clown fish. You play as the fish from birth to death, growing and avoiding sharks as well as starvation. This game won 4th place at the 2015 Tech-Olympics in Cincinnati.
